Built for Master Collectors

Three ways to collect: all the Pokémon, every version of a chosen Pokémon, or your favorite set.

  • Collect Them All

    Make your own master dex with all 1025 Pokémon, or even create a Grandmaster Collection with all the forms and variants.

  • Single Pokémon binders

    Create a binder with your favorite Pokémon and track all printings of that Pokémon, and even printings across multiple languages.

  • Complete Sets

    Track your favorite set from start to finish. Mark each card and variant as you complete the set.

Your whole collection right in your pocket

When out at events, browsing the LCS or even with friends, your collection is just a few clicks away—keep track and search up Pokémon to check versions and variants.

Card Dex

Search and browse our card database to look up printings and cards even without creating a new collection, or search a card and add it directly to your binder.

Profile & overview

Look up your profile in the app to see a list of your collections and how far you are in each of them, as well as a total overview of your overall progress.
