Built for collectors

Two ways to collect: one card per Pokémon, or every version of your favorite.

  • ◇

    Collect Them All

    One slot per Pokémon. Pick any printing and mark the exact variant you have—normal, reverse, holo, or 1st edition.

  • ◆

    Single Pokémon binders

    Create a binder for one Pokémon and track every printing across sets and languages. See progress at a glance.

  • ▣

    Card Dex

    Search the full card database. Filter by language, see all versions, and add missing cards to your collection.
