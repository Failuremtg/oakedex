Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

  • Website sign-ups: If you enter your email on our landing page, we store your email (and beta interest) so we can contact you about launch and beta access.
  • App accounts: If you create an account, your email and account data are handled through Firebase Authentication and Firestore.
  • Collection data: Your collection/binder data is stored locally on your device when signed out, and in Firestore when you sign in (for sync).
  • We don’t sell your data.

Information we collect

  • Email sign-up (website): email address and (optional) beta interest.
  • Feedback (website): message content and (optional) email if you include it.
  • Missing card submissions (website): card details, optional email, and an uploaded image of the card.
  • App account data: email/password authentication (handled by Firebase Authentication) and app data you create (stored in Firestore under your user account when signed in).
  • Local-only data: when signed out, app collection data is stored locally on your device.

How we use your information

We use information to provide app functionality (including syncing when you’re signed in), respond to feedback, and contact you if you opted in to email updates. We may also use aggregated, non-identifying information to improve the app.

Third-party services

Oakedex uses third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication and cloud data storage (Firestore), and for handling certain website submissions. See Firebase privacy and Google’s privacy policy.
  • TCGdex and PokeAPI: to fetch card and Pokémon information. Requests to these services may include basic technical data (like IP address) as part of normal internet operation.

Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists. Website submissions (like sign-ups and feedback) are kept as long as reasonably necessary to operate Oakedex and respond to requests.

Account deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data. When we receive a valid deletion request, we will delete your account within 10 days. You can use the deletion request page at /delete-account on this website, or the in-app options if available.

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, including access controls and secure transport (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children’s privacy

Oakedex is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via the Oakedex app or the contact methods listed on this website.
